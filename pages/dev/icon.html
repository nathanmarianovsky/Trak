Icon Legend

The attachment of icons next to a record name signifies the following about a record:

  • info: The record has an associated plot/synopsis which can be viewed directly by clicking on the icon.
  • panorama_fish_eye: The anime, manga, or show record has a release date in the future.
  • circle: The anime, manga, or show record is currently being released.
  • visibility: The anime or show record is currently being watched.
  • visibility_off: The anime or show record has a season that is planned to be watched.
  • bookmark: The record has been bookmarked.
